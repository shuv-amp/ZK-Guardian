# Code of Conduct

We expect respectful and professional behavior in all project spaces.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and inclusive.
- Provide constructive feedback.
- Assume good intent, ask questions when unclear.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination of any kind.
- Threats, intimidation, or abusive language.
- Sharing private information without consent.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues to maintainers via a private channel or security@zkguardian.io.
